Knife Sheath

Purchased from: John Douglas Sutherland Campbell, 9th Duke of Argyll

1887

Sheath for a knife made of hide, glass beads, brass, metal. Two panels of beadwork cover one surface, broken by a row of metal cones attached to hide thongs. One section of beadwork bears a floral design and the other smaller panel is geometric; a mostly unbroken line of bras studs edge the sheath.
